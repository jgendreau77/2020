THE PANDEMIC

In a country FAR, FAR, AWAY; A virus surfaces and is on the move as it spreads from country to country. Back here in America we just chill. Little did we know, that the virus was already here. Chaos erupts as people start to get sick and die.

As we figure out what this virus is and what to do about it. Then we give it a name of Coronavirus, and people decide not to drink Corona, lol. Then the virus is called Covid-19, a nickname! Interesting.

The virus starts to spread through America like the plague, as lockdowns near and millions run to get TOILET PAPER! WHAT? Shelves empty, stocks crash, businesses close, America put on hold.

The CDC with thier guidelines and states doing whatever they decide to do. Wear a mask and then poof those are gone and all cleaning supplies. Masks here, no masks thier, stay home.

Events all cancelled, no large gatherings, thousands and thousands around the world getting infected and dying. An election for the ages, as Biden is elected. A year like no other is coming to an end without celebration.

Lockdowns fade away and emerge in other countries as more people contract Covid-19. In America, we bounce back and forth for weeks at a time and try to attempt normality.

California suffers and nobody is helping ease the pain in the West. Now, in the mist of everything, a new variant of Covid has emerged. Now we take a deeper breathe and hope to stop the spread.
